Privacy Policy for Tackle Tanks

Last updated: January 7, 2024

This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect personal information within the game. By playing Tackle Tanks, you agree to the terms outlined below.

1. Information We Collect:

Usernames: Used for display purposes within the game. You can change your username freely on the main page.

Settings: Information related to key binds and game preferences, editable on the settings page.

Scraps: In-game currency earned by playing. Not directly editable by users; earned through gameplay.

Skins: Cosmetic items used to distinguish players. Obtained by spending Scraps, not directly editable.

Other data we collect may be neccesary to run the game, like what server area to connect to(Americas, Europe, ect) and small things like that.

2. Collection Method:

All information is automatically collected and stored using local storage in the Google Chrome browser. Users can clear their data manually through settings.

3. Purpose of Collection:

Usernames: For player identification and personalization.

Settings: Customization of in-game preferences.

Scraps and Skins: Incentives for gameplay and progression.

4. Data Security:

Protected by local storage, with programs in place to detect unauthorized alterations. Any detected breach results in a reset of all data.

5. Third-Party Services:

Currently, no third-party services are integrated. Future integration of services like Google AdMob may occur for ad display purposes. (Gotta make money some how!)

6. User Consent:

Implicit consent through browser cookie permissions. Users can manage cookie preferences in their browser settings.

7. Access and Management:

Username: Changeable on the main page with no limitations.

Settings: Editable on the settings page with no limitations.

Scraps and Skins: Not directly editable; earned through gameplay.

8. Compliance with Privacy Regulations:

Tackle Tanks complies with applicable privacy regulations, including but not limited to the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). Users may have specific rights under such regulations.

9. Future Changes:

This privacy policy may be updated in the future. Check the "Last Updated" date for the latest version. Game updates may also be updated in the future. In order to check game updates, check out the Game Guide For any questions or concerns regarding this privacy policy, contact us at tackletanks@gmail.com, or submit a issue at on Our Github.
